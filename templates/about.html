Our Story

Born at MUST, Building for Africa

KianvoSoft is a technology venture founded by three Computer Engineering students of Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST). We bring together software engineering, technical training, and applied research under a single mission: to apply technology to the real problems faced by institutions, businesses, and communities across East Africa.

Our work is organised around four interconnected pillars: Software Product Development — designing and deploying software products for education, healthcare, and small-business digital tools; Technical Training & Capacity Building — delivering short courses, bootcamps, and coding camps; Applied Research in AI — contributing to challenges in health, business, and the environment; and Technology Consultancy — helping SMEs, NGOs, and institutions with digital transformation.

The Challenge

The Problem We Are Solving

Tanzania and most East African countries face a multi-layered technology challenge that limits economic and social progress.

Lack of Local Software

Most businesses rely on expensive imported software not adapted to local needs, or manual paper-based processes. Very few homegrown companies build scalable, affordable solutions.

Digital Skills Gap

Despite growing internet access, trained software developers remain critically low. Existing training is expensive, urban-centric, and largely delivered only in English.

No Applied AI Research

AI research in Tanzania is almost non-existent — no Swahili language models, no AI-powered health tools for rural clinics, no agricultural AI for smallholder farmers.

Underserved Secondary Cities

While Dar es Salaam has growing tech activity, secondary cities like Mbeya have very little ICT support. Businesses and institutions lack access to reliable technology services.
