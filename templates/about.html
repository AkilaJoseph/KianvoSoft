Our Story

Born in Mbeya, Building for Africa

KianvoSoft is a Tanzanian-born technology company headquartered in the Mbeya Region. Founded by three passionate software developers, the company was established to address a critical gap in the Tanzanian digital ecosystem: the absence of locally-built, locally-relevant software solutions that solve everyday challenges for Tanzanian people and institutions.

Tanzania, like much of Sub-Saharan Africa, is experiencing rapid digitisation. Mobile penetration is high, the government is investing in digital infrastructure, and a young population hungry for technology is emerging. Yet, most software solutions in use are imported, expensive, and poorly adapted to local languages, workflows, and economic realities. KianvoSoft exists to change that.

The Challenge

The Problem We Are Solving

Tanzania and most African countries face a multi-layered technology challenge that limits economic and social progress.

Lack of Local Software

Most businesses rely on expensive imported software not adapted to local needs, or manual paper-based processes. Very few homegrown companies can build scalable, affordable solutions.

Digital Skills Gap

Despite growing internet access, trained software developers remain critically low. Existing training is expensive, urban-centric, and largely delivered only in English.

No Applied AI Research

AI research in Tanzania is almost non-existent — no Swahili language models, no agricultural AI for smallholder farmers, no AI-powered health tools for rural clinics.

Fragmented ICT in Secondary Cities

While Dar es Salaam has some tech activity, secondary cities like Mbeya have very little ICT support. Businesses and schools lack reliable networking and connected infrastructure.
