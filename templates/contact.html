FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and how we work.

01

What products does KianvoSoft offer?

We have built and deployed several products including Imforia (pharmacy management), Mjenzi (hardware POS), ShopManagerPro (SME management), FleetLink (logistics), IMS (impact management), Kianvo Meet (video conferencing), Kianvo Classroom (LMS), and HELP (health education portal).

02

How do I request a quote for a software project?

Fill out the contact form above with your project details, or reach out via WhatsApp or phone. We'll respond within 24 hours with a detailed proposal and quote.

03

What training programmes do you offer?

We offer short courses, bootcamps, and coding camps in programming, web/mobile development, AI/ML, and emerging technologies for students and working professionals. Training is available in Swahili and English.

04

Do you conduct AI research?

Yes. Our active research projects include brain stroke lesion detection from medical imaging and automated malaria detection through computer vision. We collaborate with MUST and other academic partners.

05

Can you work with existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialise in integrating with existing systems, APIs, and databases. We also offer digital transformation consultancy and can modernise legacy systems.

06

Where are you based and who are the founders?

We are headquartered in Mbeya, Tanzania, founded by three Computer Engineering students at Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST): Datius Rweyemamu Daud (Research & AI), Akila Body Joseph (Product & Engineering), and Aneth Alphonce Seleli (Business & Operations).
