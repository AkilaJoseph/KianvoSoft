Research & Innovation

Applied AI Research for Africa

We conduct applied AI research with practical contributions to challenges in health, business, and the environment affecting the East African region.

Brain Stroke Lesion Detection

An active research project applying artificial intelligence to support faster and more accessible stroke diagnosis in low-resource hospital settings. Leveraging medical imaging and deep learning to reduce diagnosis time in rural Tanzania.

Automated Malaria Detection

Applied research on the automated identification of malaria parasites in blood samples using AI-powered computer vision. Long-term goal of strengthening diagnostic capacity in rural health facilities across Tanzania.

In collaboration with Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST) and academic partners.

Why KianvoSoft

Our Unfair Advantage

KianvoSoft occupies a distinctive position in the East African market that is difficult for foreign competitors to replicate.

  • Locally Built: Solutions designed by Tanzanians for Tanzanian workflows, regulations, and social norms
  • Swahili Language: We build and teach in Swahili, reaching a wider population
  • University Roots: Founded at MUST — deeply connected to research, academia, and emerging talent
  • Products in Active Use: Imforia, Mjenzi, and other systems deployed with real Tanzanian clients
  • Affordable & Relevant: We understand local purchasing power without sacrificing quality
  • Community Trust: Local founders building relationships external firms cannot replicate
