Our Four Pillars

Software, Training, Research & Consultancy

KianvoSoft's work is organised around four interconnected pillars: software product development, technical training and capacity building, applied research in artificial intelligence, and technology consultancy. Together, they form a complete approach to digital transformation in East Africa.

Software Product Development

We design and deploy software products that solve real problems for institutions, businesses, and communities — with a current focus on education technology, healthcare, and small-business digital tools.

Our Products

  • Kianvo Classroom — Learning Management System for universities
  • HELP — Health Education Learning Portal for CPD
  • Imforia — Pharmacy management system (live in Tanzanian pharmacies)
  • Mjenzi — POS & inventory for hardware retailers
  • ShopManagerPro — Business management for SMEs

Applied Research in AI

We conduct applied AI research with practical contributions to challenges in health, business, and the environment affecting the East African region.
